About Me

I am doing my master's degree in Information Technology at the University of Melbourne. My study focuses on Artificial Intelligence (AI). I have completed my master's thesis with the focus on natural language processing. Specifically, I proposed a multi-modal Entity Linker that automatically detects the target and receptacle object from natural language commands. It is also capable of identifying visual and textual co-reference relations.

Upon finishing my research project, I worked as a casual research assistant on the project 'Humanoid robotic arm control through verbal commands' at UniMelb...

Moving forward, I would like to pursue a career in academia. I'm particularly interested in natural language processing, and hoping to apply it in business sectors. I am actively looking for PHD opportunities in NLP, and can start as early as July 2022.
